We are building Pengram as a software only solution, using platform-agnostic CV/AR techniques so that it supports a wide range of AR/VR devices. Given the market saturation for VR/AR hardware, and their rapid development, our clients will be able to buy any type of hardware depending on their special needs and port our software for the same experience. As these hardware progresses dramastically, we can keep up with trend and enjoy benefit from latest technology.
